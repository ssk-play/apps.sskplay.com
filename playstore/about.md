## About the developer

sskplay is a one-developer studio making small tools to make your day a little easier — the kind of things that quietly fix the small frictions you'd stopped noticing because you assumed they couldn't be fixed.

Every app does one thing well and then gets out of your way.

Currently shipping:
- App Pin — pin apps to the home screen so shortcuts survive uninstalls.
- AirPlay Touch — an AirPlay receiver where the mirrored screen is touch-interactive.
- AudioCast — stream your Mac's system audio to your Android phone over Wi-Fi.

Browse everything at apps.sskplay.com.
